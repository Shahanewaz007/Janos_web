EasyAM-HCM Human Capital Management

Now the world recognizes HUMAN RESOURCES as the CAPITAL like other capital resources deployed in an organization which manufactures products or services of value yielding in different units from total organizational human capital in malleable capacity which now-a-days may be computed in book of accounts. EasyAM-HCM is an integrated module of GMT - Group Management Technologies manage and evaluate human resources of an organization with its locally and globally operate-able features :

  • CONTROL PANEL option managing users, provides system utilities & global evaluation from HQ,
  • ORGANIZE option defines working groups of organizations, evaluation scaling, planning & control unctionalities for operations and evaluations
  • EMPLOYEES option manages database of total human resources for electronic employee filing with organization of data in different categories including psychoanalysis
  • ATTENDANCE option processes shifts, overtime, open activities in different ways e.g. local PC (manual), web, other integrated port reading hardware â€“ Biometric, RF, Barcode etc. keeping local or/and global storage of attendance data, consecutive days shifting solution, automated overtime recording, customized time recording
  • APPLICATION facilitates leaves of different type including without pay & short leave, general weekend days & holidays, adjustable closed days, closed days with pay, grievance, transfer & others
  • PENALIZE option deducting salaries, deducting working time, deducting vacations, suspension, OSD, Termination
  • SALARIES option processes and produces salary & bonus related RRSD (Reports, Registers, Schedule & Documents),
  • BENEFITS option keeps records of pre-defined unlimited service benefits e.g. salary increment, bonus, incentives, promotion, provident fund, gratuity, workers participation fund, group insurance, other rewards etc. including their computed registers, ledgers and periodic benefit documents/li>
  • EVALUATION option records periodic pre-defined evaluating working activities in the scale of performance both automated & manual.
  • REPORTS option facilitates mostly required human resources management RRSDs.
